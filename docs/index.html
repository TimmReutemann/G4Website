Soldier

A soldier follows his commands and his commands are patrol and eradicate. Thus try to avoid his gaze. You are not defenseless against his bullets! Use right click and send the package back to the sender.

Sentinel

The flying sentinels do not attack on their own. They are highly explosive creatures with a short fuse. Try pulling them with strg when selected or pull towards them. Repelling them with right click might be the wisest decision if you donÂ´t want to make a mess.

Orb

These mysterious orbs have only one purpose. You can target them and pull yourself towards them enabling you to reach grounds you otherwise would not be able to.

Portal

Portals are distortions in the time-space-continuum. They will instantly teleport you to other dimensions when clicking on their header. Explore everything you can as long as they are stable!
